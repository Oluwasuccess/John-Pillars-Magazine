Photography

Photograph of trainees during sections.


We preserve the important event and people in our lives. The ceremonies of birth and birthdays marriages and anniversaries, holidays and new houses are all recorded because they matter. It is a personal story, a timeline in our last field with faces and places that we love.
Photography is a language, though complex but our work expresses joy and sorrow, wonder and sympathy, every human emotion can find a place in photography.
Photography is more than a documentation process; it is an art form and should be treated with such reverence as it requires some level of skill and creativity.
JPI Vocational Institute has produced professional photographers over the last one year, that have been able to apply what they've learnt so far during events organized by the institute.
There have also emerged students who has gone ahead to train others with the same skill and are making impact in the field.


Encouragement:
To every young person or old out there who will like to make this field a profession, it is a beautiful field with a lot of opportunities that can be jumped at, because every great event needs to be documented and can only be done through the art of professional photography.
