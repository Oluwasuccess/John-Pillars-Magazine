People want to wear the best in form of clothes and based on our traditional culture Adire is the best traditional wear that comes from our own creativity, innovation and outstanding traditional affairs.
Adire is important in preserving our traditional African heritage in terms of custom, culture and style and anyone that see Adire on you we say you are from Africa Nigeria to be precise.

John Pillars innovation is mind-blowing because people who had zero ideas of what we are saying now we talk about adire and tie and dye, they are amazed about the practicals they're doing and the experience they are gaining. This has expose people to see different experts where they can obtain skills.
John Pillars innovation has being helpful in empowering people leading many novice to a journey of discovery.
It has also shown people that they can make wears, build a career and an entrepreneurship skill for themselves.
They can also have a business foundation of their own, training more people.
