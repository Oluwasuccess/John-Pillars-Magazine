John Pillars Innovation is an Organization in the body of Christ where we people to discover, build and empower kingdom minded people and also to bring spiritual manifestation to reality in:
- Media
- Business
- Art & Entertainment
- Religion
- Education
The John pillars Innovation Vocational Institute also known as JPI Vocational Institute began in the year 2020, when the founders saw God desiring to pour His creative Spirit on entrepreneurs and also saw many people out there desiring to learn without having financial capacity.
He arose to solve a problem by starting the institute that trains entrepreneurs at an almost free price.
The first training started on the 7th of September and ended on the 19th of September of the same year in Ibadan Oyo state. The training lasted for 2 weeks with over 50 students in attendance.
The institute in her attempt to solve problems in other community and state moved further to Kwara State, Ilorin to be precise and started another round of her training on the 16th of November which ended on the 28 of November 2020. Again this training lasted 2 weeks with over 40 students as participants.


The last training which is ongoing in Ibadan Oyo state has 30 students in attendance. This is to span for 4 weeks, which began on the 2nd of May and is scheduled to end on the 28th of May, 2021. Over 120 students have graduated from the institute with 40% actively utilizing the skill obtained from the training.
John Pillars Innovation Vocational lnstitute is a branch of John Pillars Innovation which constitute JPI Project Management company, JPI Media House and JPI Retreat Center.
